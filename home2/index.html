25 Years of Mercury:

What Makes Us Special?

Mercury isn’t just another public speaking club in Berlin.

It’s a living, breathing community with a heartbeat you can feel the moment you walk through the door. For 25 years, Mercury has been the place where people from all over the world come together—not just to speak, but to connect, grow, and inspire.

The Vibe

At our club, we bring a dynamic and energetic vibe to every gathering. We’re all about making each session fun, engaging, and a little different. That’s why we switch up our venues, meeting in cozy bars, beautiful parks, and other exciting spots around the city


Weekly Meetings​

Consistency is key! We’re the only club in Berlin that offers weekly meetings, giving you more opportunities to practice, improve, and connect with others. Whether you’re looking to sharpen your speaking skills or simply meet new people, we’re here for you every week.
.

Social Events

We host regular social events that are open to everyone, whether you’re a member or just curious. Join us and experience the friendly, inclusive atmosphere that makes our club so special.

Hybrid Meetings

We’re proud to offer hybrid meetings in Berlin, giving you the flexibility to join us in person or online. Whether you’re nearby or far away, our hybrid approach makes it easy for everyone to stay connected and participate fully.
